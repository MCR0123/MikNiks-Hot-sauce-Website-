Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I place an order?
You can place an order directly through our website by browsing our products, adding them to your cart, and proceeding to checkout. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete your purchase.

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, and cash on delivery

Can I modify my order after placing it?
If you need to modify your order, please contact us as soon as possible. Once an order is processed, we may not be able to make changes.

What are your shipping options?
Currently we only allow collection from the store, no delivery.

Do you ship internationally?
No, we do not ship internationally.Only local and collection.

Are your products gluten-free?
Yes, all our hot sauces are gluten-free.

Do you offer vegan options?
Yes, our hot sauces are vegan-friendly and do not contain any animal products.

What is the shelf life of your hot sauces?
Our hot sauces have a shelf life of 12 months when stored in a cool, dry place. Once opened, we recommend consuming them within 6 months for the best flavor.

What is your return policy?
We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. The product must be unopened and in its original packaging. Please contact us to initiate a return.

How do I request a refund?
To request a refund, please contact our customer service team with your order details. Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days.

Who pays for return shipping?
The customer is responsible for return shipping costs unless the product was damaged or incorrect upon arrival.

How do I create an account?
Click on the "Sign Up" button at the top of the page and fill in the required information. Follow the instructions to complete the registration process.

How do I reset my password?
Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and enter your email address. We will send you a link to reset your password.
